Fast Food and Eating Out Rules

General:
- Protein first
- Sauce separate when possible
- Choose one carb source
- Avoid stacking fries + dessert + sugary drinks
- Water or zero drink

Burger place:
- Burger is okay
- Avoid extra bacon / extra cheese if cutting
- Small fries only if planned
- No milkshake

Belgian cuisine:
- Best choices: steak nature, chicken, mussels nature
- Sauce separate
- Choose small fries or potatoes, not both
- Avoid heavy cream sauces when possible

After fast food:
- Next meal should be lean protein + vegetables
- Do not panic or compensate aggressively
- Track it and continue normally
