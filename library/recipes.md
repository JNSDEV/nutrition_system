# Recipes

1. Protein Overnight Oats
Jonas:
- 226-260 g skyr
- 30 g whey
- 40 g oats
- 100 ml semi-skimmed milk
- Fruit

Partner:
- 150 g skyr
- 30 g whey
- 20-30 g oats
- 40-100 ml semi-skimmed milk
- Fruit

2. Cottage Cheese Protein Pancakes
- 200 g cottage cheese
- 2 eggs
- 40 g oats
- 30 g whey
- 60 ml milk
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- Salt
Optional: berries

3. Protein Pasta + Tofu + Cottage Cheese Tomato Sauce
For 4 portions:
- 400 g dry protein pasta
- 200 g tofu
- 300 g cottage cheese
- 70-80 g tomato paste
- 300-350 ml water
- Spices

Important:
- Blend cottage cheese first
- Add cottage cheese after cooking, on low heat or off heat
- Do not boil after adding cottage cheese

4. Chili Con Carne
For 4 portions:
- 400 g lean ground beef
- 2 cans kidney beans
- 2 cans chopped tomatoes
- Onion, garlic, paprika
- Chili spices, cumin, salt, pepper

5. Healthy Chocolate Cake
Whole cake:
- 200 g skyr
- 2 eggs
- 40 g oats
- 30 g whey
- 20 g cocoa powder
- 50 g unsweetened apple sauce
- 20 g sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- Salt

Bake:
- 180 C
- 25-30 minutes
