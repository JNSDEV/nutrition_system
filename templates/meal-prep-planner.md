# Meal Prep Planner

Meal Prep Planner

Goal:
- Cook once, eat twice
- Dinner provides next-day office lunch

Rules:
- Cook 4 portions for dinner
- Portion immediately after cooking
- Label containers: Jonas / Farva / Lunch
- Store lunch containers in fridge immediately

Best meal prep meals:
- Chicken + rice + vegetables
- Chicken + sweet potato + vegetables
- Protein pasta + tofu + cottage cheese sauce
- Chili con carne

Office lunch requirements:
- Reheats well
- Does not need extra cooking
- Protein included
- Carb included
- Avoid messy sauces unless packed separately

Weekly prep checklist:
- Prepare oats for breakfast
- Cook dinner in 4 portions
- Pack lunch before bed
- Put shaker / fruit in bag
- Keep emergency snack available
