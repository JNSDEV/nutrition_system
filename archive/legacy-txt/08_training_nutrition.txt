Training Nutrition

Z2 60 min:
- Normal meal before is usually enough
- Optional banana before
- Water or electrolytes during
- No gel needed unless under-fueled

Z2 90-120 min:
- Eat carbs before
- During: 30-45 g carbs per hour if needed
- Examples: gel, banana, sports drink
- Post-ride: protein + carbs

SST / Tempo 60 min:
- More carb-heavy than easy Z2
- Eat carbs 60-90 min before
- Gel optional if lunch was light
- Post-workout: rice, potatoes, pasta + lean protein

General:
- Do not under-eat on big training days
- If ride burns ~1000 kcal, add extra carbs later
- Protein target stays high
