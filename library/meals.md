# Meals

## Breakfast

### Protein Overnight Oats
**archetype:** breakfast
Skyr, whey, oats, milk, fruit — soaked overnight. See `library:recipes#protein-overnight-oats` for prep.

### Warm Protein Oats
**archetype:** breakfast
Cooked oats with whey stirred in; served warm. Quick stovetop version.

### Skyr Bowl
**archetype:** breakfast
Plain skyr with fruit or granola. No prep required.

### Cottage Cheese Protein Pancakes
**archetype:** breakfast
Cottage cheese, eggs, oats, whey pancake batter. See `library:recipes#cottage-cheese-protein-pancakes` for prep.

## Lunch

### Chicken + Rice + Vegetables
**archetype:** lunch
(Also served as leftover lunch from the previous night's dinner cook.)

### Protein Pasta + Tofu + Cottage Cheese Sauce
**archetype:** lunch
(Also served as leftover lunch from the previous night's dinner cook.) See `library:recipes#protein-pasta-tofu-cottage-cheese-tomato-sauce` for sauce prep.

### Chili Con Carne
**archetype:** lunch
(Also served as leftover lunch from the previous night's dinner cook.) See `library:recipes#chili-con-carne` for prep.

### Cottage Cheese Pancakes
**archetype:** lunch
Same batter as breakfast version; served as a savoury lunch option.

### Skyr Bowl with Chicken
**archetype:** lunch
Plain skyr, chicken breast, chickpeas, dried tomatoes, greens, and dressing. No cooking.

### Wholegrain Bread with Chicken or Ham
**archetype:** lunch
Wholegrain bread with chicken breast or lean ham. No cooking.

## Dinner

### Chicken + Rice + Vegetables (Dinner)
**archetype:** dinner
Cook 4 portions: 2 for tonight, 2 for next-day lunch. See `library/portions.md` for Jonas vs Farva portion weights.

### Chicken + Potatoes or Sweet Potatoes + Vegetables
**archetype:** dinner
Cook 4 portions. Roast or airfryer potatoes/sweet potatoes alongside chicken.

### Protein Pasta + Tofu + Cottage Cheese Tomato Sauce
**archetype:** dinner
Cook 4 portions. See `library:recipes#protein-pasta-tofu-cottage-cheese-tomato-sauce` for sauce handling — do not boil after adding cottage cheese.

### Chili Con Carne with Lean Beef
**archetype:** dinner
Cook 4 portions. See `library:recipes#chili-con-carne`.

### Beef Mince with Bread, Potatoes or Pasta
**archetype:** dinner
Cook 4 portions. Versatile base — pair with bread, roast potatoes, or pasta.

## Snacks

**archetype:** snack

### Banana
**archetype:** snack
Fast carb + potassium. No prep.

### Fruit
**archetype:** snack
Seasonal fruit. No prep.

### Rice Cakes

Plain rice cakes. No prep. Low-calorie volume option.
