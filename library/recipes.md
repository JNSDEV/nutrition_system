# Recipes

## Breakfast Recipes

### Protein Overnight Oats

**Jonas:**

**ingredients:**
- 226-260 g skyr
- 30 g whey
- 40 g oats
- 100 ml semi-skimmed milk
- fruit (to taste)

**Farva:**

**ingredients:**
- 150 g skyr
- 30 g whey
- 20-30 g oats
- 40-100 ml semi-skimmed milk
- fruit (to taste)

**notes:** Soak overnight in the fridge. Stir in the morning before eating.

### Cottage Cheese Protein Pancakes

**ingredients:**
- 200 g cottage cheese
- 2 eggs
- 40 g oats
- 30 g whey
- 60 ml milk
- 1/2 tsp baking powder
- salt (pinch)

**notes:** Optional: add berries to batter. Fry on medium heat; 2–3 minutes per side.

## Dinner Recipes

### Protein Pasta + Tofu + Cottage Cheese Tomato Sauce

Serves 4 portions.

**ingredients:**
- 400 g dry protein pasta
- 200 g tofu
- 300 g cottage cheese
- 70-80 g tomato paste
- 300-350 ml water
- spices (salt, pepper, Italian herbs to taste)

**notes:**
- Blend cottage cheese to a smooth sauce before adding.
- Add cottage cheese off heat or on very low heat.
- Do not boil after adding cottage cheese — it will split.

### Chili Con Carne

Serves 4 portions.

**ingredients:**
- 400 g lean ground beef
- 2 cans kidney beans (drained)
- 2 cans chopped tomatoes
- 1 onion (diced)
- 3 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 bell pepper (diced)
- chili powder, cumin, paprika, salt, pepper (to taste)

## Snack Recipes

### Healthy Chocolate Cake

Whole cake (slice into portions).

**ingredients:**
- 200 g skyr
- 2 eggs
- 40 g oats
- 30 g whey
- 20 g cocoa powder
- 50 g unsweetened apple sauce
- 20 g sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- salt (pinch)

**notes:** Bake at 180°C for 25–30 minutes. Toothpick test for doneness.
