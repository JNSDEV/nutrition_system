Cooking Rules

Airfryer:
- Chicken: 180 C for 10-18 min depending on thickness
- Chickpeas: 190 C for 12-15 min
- No aluminium for crisping
- Use aluminium only if catching very wet marinade or sauce
- Dry chickpeas well before airfrying

Whey:
- Do not heat whey directly
- Add whey after oats cool 1-2 minutes
- This prevents clumping

Cottage cheese:
- Blend first for smooth sauce
- Add after cooking
- Keep heat low or off
- Do not boil after adding

Pancakes:
- If batter is too wet, add 10-20 g oats
- Frozen fruit can make batter wet
- Bake slower on medium-low heat

Protein oats:
- Cook oats first
- Cool slightly
- Stir in whey after
