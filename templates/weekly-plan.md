# Weekly Meal Plan

Weekly Meal Plan for 2 People

Fixed breakfast every day:
- Protein overnight oats

Monday:
Lunch:
- Bread + chicken / lean ham
Dinner:
- Chicken + rice + vegetables
Cook 4 portions

Tuesday:
Lunch:
- Leftover chicken + rice
Dinner:
- Protein pasta + tofu + cottage cheese tomato sauce
Cook 4 portions

Wednesday:
Lunch:
- Leftover chicken + rice
Dinner:
- Chili con carne
Cook 4 portions

Thursday:
Lunch:
- Leftover protein pasta
Dinner:
- Chicken + sweet potato + vegetables
Cook 4 portions

Friday:
Lunch:
- Leftover chili
Dinner:
- Protein pasta + tofu
Cook 4 portions

Saturday:
Lunch:
- Leftover chicken + sweet potato
Dinner:
- Flex meal: chicken, beef, pasta, or eating out

Sunday:
Lunch:
- Leftover protein pasta
Dinner:
- Chicken + rice + vegetables
Cook extra for Monday if needed
