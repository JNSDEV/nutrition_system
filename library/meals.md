# Meals

Breakfast:
- Protein overnight oats
- Warm protein oats
- Skyr bowl
- Cottage cheese protein pancakes

Lunch:
- Leftover chicken + rice + vegetables
- Leftover protein pasta + tofu + cottage cheese sauce
- Leftover chili con carne
- Cottage cheese pancakes
- Skyr bowl with chicken, chickpeas, dried tomatoes, greens and dressing
- Wholegrain bread with chicken breast or lean ham

Dinner:
- Chicken + rice + vegetables
- Chicken + potatoes or sweet potatoes + vegetables
- Protein pasta + tofu + cottage cheese tomato sauce
- Chili con carne with lean beef
- Beef mince with bread, potatoes or pasta

Snacks / dessert:
- Healthy chocolate cake
- Protein ice cream
- Skyr + oats
- Whey shake
- Banana
- Fruit
- Rice cakes
