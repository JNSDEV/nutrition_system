## Decisions Made

- **Bootstrap-time `node_modules/` gitignore** — Added to `backend/.gitignore` at scaffold time so plan 05-03 (which `npm install`s for the seed script) cannot accidentally commit deps.
- **Per-function `verify_jwt` policy** — proxy-anthropic and github-fs require auth (`verify_jwt=true`); only health is public (`verify_jwt=false`).
- **DB password via env var, not interactive prompt** — `SUPABASE_DB_PASSWORD='<provided>' supabase link …` keeps the password out of shell history and out of any committed file.
- **README documents ES256 fallback up-front** — per the phase research risk register, so a future 401 after deploy is diagnosable without re-opening research notes.
