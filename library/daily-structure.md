# Daily Structure

Breakfast:
- Protein overnight oats or warm protein oats
- Skyr + whey + oats + milk + fruit

Lunch:
- Prefer leftovers from previous dinner
- Must be portable for office days

Dinner:
- Cook 4 portions whenever possible
- 2 portions for dinner
- 2 portions for next-day lunch

Snacks:
- Skyr
- Whey shake
- Banana
- Rice cakes
- Protein ice cream
- Healthy chocolate cake
