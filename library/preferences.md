# Preferences

Preferred foods:
- Chicken
- Skyr
- Whey
- Oats
- Protein pasta
- Cottage cheese
- Lean beef / pure beef mince
- Tofu
- Chickpeas
- Potatoes and sweet potatoes
- Blueberries
- Bananas

Foods to avoid or limit:
- Heavy sauces
- Excess mayo
- Fast food unless planned
- Pastries as regular snacks
- Extra oil unless tracked

Known dislikes:
- No fish
- No feta

Helpful habits:
- High protein breakfast
- Meal prep leftovers for office lunch
- Use zero-calorie sauces when useful
- Keep sauces separate when eating out
